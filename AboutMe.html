About Me

My Story

Before transitioning into IT, I worked as a Field Operations Lead at TruGreen Lawn Care for five years. During that time, I built strong leadership and problem-solving skills and was consistently recognized as a top candidate for advancement into management.

Despite enjoying my role, I knew I wanted to push myself further and pursue a career with greater long-term growth opportunities. That decision led me to enroll at Thaddeus Stevens College of Technology, where I am currently studying Computer and Network Systems Administration.

I have truly enjoyed my experience so far and take pride in maintaining a 4.0 GPA while developing hands-on technical skills. I have also been fortunate to begin gaining real-world experience as an IT Support Technician while in school.

Professional Skills

  • Technical Support & Troubleshooting: Diagnose and resolve hardware, software, and operating system issues.
  • Networking Fundamentals: Knowledge of TCP/IP, local area networking, and connectivity troubleshooting.
  • Systems Administration: Experience with Windows Server environments, maintenance, backups, and recovery.
  • PC Hardware & Desktop Support: Install, configure, upgrade, and maintain computer systems.
  • Problem-Solving: Analyze issues, identify root causes, and implement effective solutions.
  • Communication: Provide clear, professional support to end users and team members.
  • Leadership: Experience leading teams, coordinating work, and supporting coworkers.
  • Project Coordination: Organized when managing tasks, schedules, and documentation.
  • Continuous Learning: Quickly learn new technologies and adapt to changing environments.

Goals

My primary goal is to continue learning and growing every day, both technically and professionally. I am driven to build a career in IT where I can make a meaningful impact and contribute to an organization’s success.

As I gain experience, I aim to develop into roles such as Systems Administrator or Sales Engineer. Long-term, I am also interested in transitioning into a leadership or management position.

Personal
