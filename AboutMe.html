About Me

My Story

Before transitioning into IT, I spent five years in operations leadership at TruGreen Lawn Care, where I developed strong leadership, customer service, and problem-solving skills while managing field operations and supporting team performance.

Although successful in that role, I wanted to pursue a more technical and growth-oriented career path. That decision led me to enroll at Thaddeus Stevens College of Technology in the Computer and Network Systems Administration program.

Since beginning that journey, I have maintained a 4.0 GPA while gaining hands-on enterprise IT experience as an IT Support Technician supporting a live healthcare and senior living environment.

Goals

My goal is to continue building technical expertise in systems administration, networking, cloud technologies, and enterprise infrastructure while growing into increasingly technical and impactful IT roles.

Long term, I aspire to advance into positions such as Systems Administrator, Infrastructure Engineer, or Sales Engineer, with eventual interest in technical leadership and management.

Personal

I live in Hershey, Pennsylvania with my wife and daughter. Outside of work and school, I enjoy spending time with family, watching sports, fishing, and staying active in continuous professional development.
